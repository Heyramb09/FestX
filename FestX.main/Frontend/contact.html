Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for TechFest 2025?
Registration for TechFest 2025 can be completed through our website. Navigate to the Register page, fill out the registration form, and follow the payment instructions if applicable.
What are the dates for TechFest 2025?
TechFest 2025 will take place from April 15-18, 2025. Check our Schedule page for detailed timings of all events and workshops.
Can I participate in multiple events?
Yes, you can participate in multiple events as long as there are no scheduling conflicts. Make sure to check the schedule before registering for multiple events.
Are there any accommodation facilities available?
Yes, we provide accommodation facilities for outstation participants. You can select the accommodation option during the registration process.
Is there a refund policy if I cancel my registration?
Refunds are available if cancellation is made at least 14 days before the event. A processing fee of 10% will be deducted. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made less than 14 days before the event.
