About The Fest

Celebrating creativity, innovation, and excellence - our annual college cultural and technical extravaganza

Fest Theme

2025 Edition

Digital Renaissance

This year's theme "Digital Renaissance" celebrates the fusion of traditional art forms with cutting-edge technology. Just as the Renaissance period marked a rebirth of art, culture, and knowledge, our fest aims to showcase how digital technologies are transforming creative expression and innovation.

Through various events, competitions, and exhibitions, participants will explore how artificial intelligence, virtual reality, blockchain, and other emerging technologies are reshaping our approach to art, music, literature, and scientific discovery.

Join us in this exciting journey where the past meets the future, and where innovation is guided by the timeless principles of human creativity and expression.
